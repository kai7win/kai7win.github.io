AIBizCard App - Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 19, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes Our policies and procedures regarding the AIBizCard App and tells You about Your privacy rights and how the law protects You.

Business Card Recognition and AI Processing

Complete Privacy Guarantee: AIBizCard App is designed with your privacy as our top priority. We DO NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any of your data.

Camera and Image Processing

To provide business card recognition functionality, our app requires access to your device's camera. We use advanced AI models to analyze business cards and extract contact information.

100% On-Device Processing:

  • All AI models run entirely on your device
  • Business card recognition is performed offline
  • No internet connection required for core functionality
  • All processing happens locally on your iPhone/iPad

Zero Data Collection:

  • No Photo Storage: We do not save, store, or keep any photos of your business cards
  • No Contact Data Collection: Extracted contact information is not transmitted to our servers
  • No Analytics: We do not collect usage data or analytics
  • No Tracking: We do not track your behavior or app usage patterns

How It Works

When you scan a business card:

  1. The camera captures the image temporarily in device memory
  2. Our offline AI model processes the image to recognize text and layout
  3. Contact information is extracted and displayed to you
  4. The temporary image is immediately discarded from memory
  5. You can choose to save the contact information to your device's Contacts app

No Data Transmission: At no point during this process is any data sent to external servers, third parties, or our company. Everything happens entirely within your device.

Contact Storage

If you choose to save extracted contact information:

  • Data is saved directly to your device's built-in Contacts app
  • We use Apple's standard Contacts framework
  • This data remains under your control and Apple's privacy policies
  • We do not have access to your saved contacts
