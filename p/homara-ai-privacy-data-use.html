Homara AI - Privacy and Data Use

Last updated: April 5, 2026

AI generation: Images and prompts that you choose to submit may be sent to Replicate-powered services for text-to-image and image-to-image generation.

Image retention: Generated images are not retained on our servers and are automatically deleted after the AI generation is completed.

Tracking permission: Tracking data is sent only if you grant AppTrackingTransparency permission. If you do not allow tracking, no tracking data is sent.

Privacy Summary

Data Used for AI Processing

User-provided images and prompts only

Room photos, reference images, style selections, and text prompts that you choose to provide may be processed to generate design results.

Data Retention

Auto-deleted after generation

Generated images are not kept on our servers after the generation workflow finishes.

Tracking and Advertising

Permission-based only

If you authorize AppTrackingTransparency, tracking may be used to improve user experience, measure marketing performance, and support advertising attribution. If you decline, tracking data is not sent.

How Data Is Used

  • Uploaded room images: Photos or images that you choose to upload may be sent for AI analysis and generation so Homara AI can produce design, declutter, or transformation results.
  • Text prompts and style choices: Prompts, room types, styles, palettes, and related settings may be sent with your request to guide the generated output.
  • Purpose limitation: Submitted content is used only to provide the image generation or editing result you requested.
  • Automatic deletion: AI-generated image outputs are automatically deleted after processing and are not retained on our servers.
  • ATT permission handling: Tracking is used only when you explicitly grant AppTrackingTransparency permission. If permission is not granted, no tracking data is transmitted for tracking purposes.

Third-Party AI Provider

When you use Homara AI features that require AI generation, the images and prompts you submit may be processed through Replicate-powered text-to-image and image-to-image services in order to generate results. That processing is necessary for the app's core functionality.

Tracking Permission and Advertising Attribution

If you allow AppTrackingTransparency permission, Homara AI may use tracking-related data to improve the user experience, measure marketing performance, understand app discovery, and support advertising attribution. If you do not allow this permission, Homara AI does not send tracking data for those purposes.

Children's Privacy

Homara AI does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 for tracking without the required permission flow.

Changes to This Page

If our privacy or data-use practices change, we will update this page and revise the date shown above.
