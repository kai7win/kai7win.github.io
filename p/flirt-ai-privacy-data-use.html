Flirt AI - Privacy and Data Use

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Tracking status: No Tracking

AI processing: User-submitted text and conversation screenshots may be sent to OpenAI models to analyze the conversation context and generate reply suggestions.

Core promise: Flirt AI uses your input only to provide the requested suggestion and does not use it for advertising or cross-app tracking.

Privacy Summary

Data Used to Track You

None

Flirt AI does not track users across apps, websites, or third-party services.

Data Sent for AI Processing

User-provided input only

Text and conversation screenshots that you choose to provide in the app may be transmitted to OpenAI in order to analyze them and return suggestion results.

Advertising and Analytics SDKs

Not used for tracking

Flirt AI does not use ad networks, behavioral profiling tools, or third-party analytics SDKs for tracking.

How Data Is Used

  • User input: Messages, profile details, or other text that you voluntarily enter may be sent to OpenAI so the app can generate flirting advice or suggested replies.
  • Conversation screenshots: Screenshots that you choose to upload may also be sent to OpenAI so the app can analyze the conversation and provide context-aware suggestions.
  • Purpose limitation: This data is used only to provide the analysis and suggestion you requested.
  • No tracking: We do not use your input to build ad profiles or track you across other apps or websites.
  • No advertising identifiers: We do not use IDFA or similar identifiers for ad targeting.

Third-Party AI Provider

When you use Flirt AI features that require AI analysis, the content you submit, including text inputs and conversation screenshots, is processed by OpenAI as a service provider to generate results. That processing is necessary for the app's core functionality.

Children's Privacy

Flirt AI does not include user-tracking mechanisms for any users, including children under 13.

Changes to This Page

If our privacy or data-use practices change, we will update this page and revise the date shown above.
